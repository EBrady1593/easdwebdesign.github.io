Interesting facts about the internet

  1. In the early 1970's Vint Cerf and Bob Kahn began working on the design of what we know as the internet.
  2. Their work was based upon work by Paul Baren who was developing a communication network for the military that could withstand a nuclear strike and still deliver messages reliably.
  3. Today, no single person or group controls the internet.
  4. It is made up of many, many smaller networks who strive to collaborate so that their networks can communicate with all the other networks.
  5. It is this interdependency that makes everyone involved work together to make the internet as stable and reliable as possible. You as a private citizen access the internet through your service provider, or ISP (internet service provider).

Interesting Facts about the World Wide Web

  • The inventor of the World Wide Web is Sir Tim Berners-Lee who was born and lived in England.
  • Berners-Lee attended Oxford University.
  • The idea of the web came to hime while he was working at CERN, the European Organization for Nuclear Research. They operate the largest partical physics lab in the world.
  • The work and the value of what Berners-Lee has done lad him to be knighted by Queen Elizabeth in 2004.
  • One of the most interesting facts about Berners-Lee is that he has never profitted directly by his work on developing the web. He has chosen to put the benefits for others about his own finanical gain.
